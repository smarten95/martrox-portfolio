IDEA:

The site is called "NemSkat" which translates to "EasyTaxes".

The idea was to make an automatic accounting system on an App for your phone. This app was to help you correct your preliminary income assessment.

The way to do this was to have an automatic system driven by an algorithm. This algorithm uses photos of your monthly job payment letters and with this easy obtainable data the app will help you make sure that you're filling out the correct data, so you can avoid having to pay outstanding taxes.

PROCESS:

Xd were used to make a wireframe of the website layout.

After that we made a moodboard containing the colors, fonts and the overall theme and feeling.

Xd were then again used to make a mockup of the Mobile App and of the website promoting the app.

We used design process to have a structured idea all the way through the assignment with usability tests and experimentations being done frequently.
