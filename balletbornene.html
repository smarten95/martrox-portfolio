IDEA:

the site was for a fictional ballet dance school for kids with ADHD.

Our goal was to create a site that felt relaxed and gender neautral and was easy to navigate, with big buttons and large headings - clear messages.

With the neutral colors we aimed to attract both girls as well as boys for our dancing classes.

PROCESS:

Xd were used to make a wireframes of the website layout for both mobile and pc.

After that we made a moodboard containing the colors, fonts and the overall theme and feeling.

Xd were then again used to make a prototype of the website.

We used design process to have a structured idea all the way through the assignment with usability tests and experimentations being done frequently.
